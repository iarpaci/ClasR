## 1. PURPOSE (ONE SENTENCE)

To surface systematically absent elements —
counter-cases, alternative explanations, competing frameworks,
unacknowledged scope limits —
that are structurally expected but never opened in the manuscript.

---

## 2. DESIGN PRINCIPLE

Silence is not the same as omission.

Omission = a section or element is missing.
Silence = an element is present by implication
but never explicitly acknowledged or addressed.

This kit reads:
  not what is absent from the structure
  but what the argument implies yet never confronts.

Structural absences → NUMERICAL HAT KIT (SECTION 7)
Argumentative silences → this kit (SECTION 7 + SECTION 8)

---

## 3. SCOPE — SIGNALS READ

### 3.1 Unacknowledged Counter-Cases

The manuscript presents a pattern or finding
without acknowledging cases that contradict or complicate it.

Signal indicators:
- Single-direction results presented without variance discussion
- "All cases show..." or "consistently..." without exception handling
- Comparative framing where only one side is developed

---

### 3.2 Alternative Explanations Never Opened

A causal or interpretive claim is made
without acknowledging competing explanations.

Signal indicators:
- "X leads to Y" with no discussion of Z as alternative driver
- Theoretical framework chosen without acknowledgment
  that other frameworks exist
- Results attributed to one mechanism when multiple are plausible

---

### 3.3 Competing Frameworks Suppressed

The manuscript operates within one theoretical tradition
and treats it as the only available lens.

Signal indicators:
- Literature review cites only confirmatory sources
- Methodological choice presented as neutral or obvious
- Disciplinary assumptions never stated as assumptions

---

### 3.4 Unacknowledged Scope Limits

The manuscript's claims extend beyond what the data supports,
but the extension is never flagged as a limit.

Signal indicators:
- Local findings narrated without scope boundary
- Temporal scope of data wider than analysis acknowledges
- Subgroup findings generalized without subgroup acknowledgment

---

### 3.5 Silenced Uncertainty

Uncertainty is present in the analysis
but suppressed in the language.

Signal indicators:
- Confidence intervals present but not discussed
- Model fit statistics reported but not interpreted
- "Future work" language used to deflect rather than bound

---

## 4. WHAT THIS KIT DOES NOT DO

Does not:
  Judge whether silences are intentional
  Evaluate the quality of the argument
  Recommend additions or revisions
  Label the manuscript as incomplete

Reads only:
  Whether structurally expected confrontations are present or absent
  Whether the argument implies an alternative it never addresses

---

## 5. OUTPUT BEHAVIOR

Silence signals are reported as:

```
SILENCE DETECTED: [type — e.g., alternative explanation absent]
Location: [SECTION or argument zone where implied]
Label: UNADDRESSED ALTERNATIVE / UNACKNOWLEDGED COUNTER-CASE /
       SUPPRESSED SCOPE LIMIT / SILENCED UNCERTAINTY /
       FRAMEWORK MONOPOLY
```

No judgment word. No recommendation.
Signal + location + label only.

---

## 6. INTEGRATION — LENS-BRIDGE CONNECTION

### Primary: SECTION 7 — Structural Integrity
Systematic absences and unacknowledged counter-cases
→ reported as structural integrity notes within SECTION 7

### Secondary: SECTION 8 — Limits & Uncertainties
Silence-detected absences that constitute unmapped knowledge boundaries
→ reported as "unmapped boundary" flags within SECTION 8

Silence Lens output never opens a new section.
Always appears as a sub-signal within the relevant SECTION.

---

## 7. ACTIVATION

Automatically active in SECTION 7 for every report.
Passive scan across all SECTIONs.
Activates in SECTION 8 only when silence signals
qualify as unmapped knowledge boundaries.

---

## 8. COLLISION MANAGEMENT

If a silence signal overlaps with a LENSSA-DEPTH
or Verbal Lens signal in the same SECTION:
→ reported once, combined note
→ more specifically bound lens takes precedence
→ collision not surfaced to user

---

## 9. HARD LOCK

This kit must never:
  Label silence as deliberate or strategic
  Produce rhetoric critique
  Suggest what the author "should have" included
  Generate independent flags or scores outside SECTION 7 / SECTION 8
  Act as a standalone structural report

---

## 10. CANONICAL DEFINITION

SILENCE LENS v1.0 surfaces systematically absent elements —
counter-cases, alternative explanations, competing frameworks,
and unacknowledged scope limits — as discursive absence signals,
without producing judgment, evaluation, or recommendation.

## 1. PURPOSE (ONE SENTENCE)

To synthesize signals from across the CLASR-EN system
into a structured desk-reject risk profile —
mapping the combination of behavioral patterns
most likely to trigger early editorial rejection —
without producing a verdict or recommendation.

---

## 2. DESIGN PRINCIPLE

No single signal causes a desk rejection.
Desk rejection is a pattern event.

The Academic Structure Meta-System produces
DESK_REJECT_RISK_HIGH / MEDIUM flags from heading structure alone.
This kit operates differently:

It reads the co-occurrence and combination
of behavioral signals across the full manuscript —
claim behavior, structural completeness, scope-journal fit,
language posture, and limit acknowledgment —
and maps whether that combination produces
a recognizable desk-reject risk profile.

```
Signals aggregated from active kits
         →
Co-occurrence patterns mapped
         →
Risk profile assembled per trigger zone
         →
Synthesis reported — no verdict
```

This kit does not replace DESK_REJECT_RISK flags
from the Structural QA system.
It extends them with behavioral and rhetorical dimensions.

---

## 3. TRIGGER ZONES

Five trigger zones are mapped independently.

### 3.1 Scope–Journal Fit Signal

The manuscript's claimed scope exceeds or mismatches
what its evidence base can support for the target journal tier.

Signal co-occurrence indicators:
- PRIMACY CLAIM — UNBOUNDED (Contribution Framing Kit)
  + LOW evidence threshold (Reader Model Kit)
- SCALE DRIFT (Discussion Scope Drift Kit)
  + Q1 profile (AUTO-Q or user-declared)
- READER_PROFILE_POLICY_ADJACENT
  + specialist journal implied by Q1 target

Label: SCOPE_FIT_RISK

---

### 3.2 Abstract Posture Signal

The abstract creates expectations
the body cannot meet — or misrepresents the manuscript's
actual behavioral profile to an editor reading in 90 seconds.

Signal co-occurrence indicators:
- CLAIM ESCALATION — ABSTRACT EXCEEDS BODY
  (Abstract→Body Coherence Kit)
- CONTRIBUTION SCOPE INFLATION
  (Contribution Framing Kit)
- AUTO-Q estimate significantly higher than body supports

Label: ABSTRACT_POSTURE_RISK

---

### 3.3 Structural Completeness Signal

The manuscript is missing elements
that editors verify before sending to review.

Signal co-occurrence indicators:
- MANDATORY_MISSING (Structural QA Kit)
- SECTION 8 absent or under 3 lines
  (Calibration-Deep §1.3)
- REPLICATION STRUCTURE GAP in data-heavy manuscript
  (Replication Signal Kit)
- DEPENDENCY_BREAK in methods chain
  (Structural QA Kit)

Label: STRUCTURAL_COMPLETENESS_RISK

---

### 3.4 Language Posture Signal

The manuscript's language register signals
either over-claim or misalignment
with the target journal's editorial expectations.

Signal co-occurrence indicators:
- HIGH frequency claim words + HIGH intensity
  AMPLIFICATION CLUSTER (Calibration-Deep + LENSSA-DEPTH)
- NORMATIVE LANGUAGE detected (Calibration-Deep §1.4)
  + Q1 target
- TENSION or MISMATCH (Reader Model Kit)
  + specialist journal framing

Label: LANGUAGE_POSTURE_RISK

---

### 3.5 Integrity and Transparency Signal

The manuscript carries signals that trigger
editorial due-diligence concerns before peer review.

Signal co-occurrence indicators:
- ELEVATED SELF-CITATION DENSITY
  (Citation Behavior Kit)
- OUTCOME INSTRUMENT INTERSECTION
  (Integrity Signal Kit)
- STRUCTURAL_NULL_ABSENCE in confirmatory design
  (Negative Result Visibility Kit)
- PRE-REGISTRATION SIGNAL ABSENT
  in clinical or experimental design (Replication Signal Kit)

Label: INTEGRITY_TRANSPARENCY_RISK

## 4. RISK PROFILE ASSEMBLY

After zone mapping, a desk-reject risk profile is assembled:

```
DESK-REJECT RISK PROFILE

Scope–Journal Fit:          [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Abstract Posture:           [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Structural Completeness:    [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Language Posture:           [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Integrity & Transparency:   [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]

Active trigger zones: [N of 5]
```

No score. No ranking. No recommendation.
Zone map only.

Single zone active — LOW co-occurrence pattern
Two zones active — MODERATE co-occurrence pattern
Three or more zones active — HIGH co-occurrence pattern

These labels describe signal density, not editorial outcome.

---

Reported as a synthesis block within SECTION 0,
immediately after the AUTO-Q estimate:

```
▸ DESK-REJECT RISK PROFILE

Scope–Journal Fit:          [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Abstract Posture:           [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Structural Completeness:    [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Language Posture:           [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]
Integrity & Transparency:   [RISK DETECTED / NOT DETECTED]

Co-occurrence pattern: [LOW / MODERATE / HIGH]
```

If no trigger zones are active:
→ Single line in SECTION 0:
  [Desk-reject risk profile: no active trigger zones detected]

---

## 6. Q-VARIANT CALIBRATION

Q1:
  All five zones scanned at full sensitivity.
  Any MODERATE or HIGH co-occurrence pattern explicitly surfaced.
  Q1 editorial gatekeeping is most stringent.

Q2:
  All zones scanned at standard sensitivity.
  HIGH co-occurrence pattern explicitly surfaced.
  MODERATE noted without escalation.

Q3:
  Structural Completeness and Abstract Posture zones only
  scanned at full sensitivity.
  Language Posture and Scope–Journal Fit at reduced sensitivity.
  Integrity & Transparency at standard sensitivity.

---

## 7. WHAT THIS KIT DOES NOT DO

Does not:
  Predict editorial decisions
  Evaluate journal fit for a specific named journal
  Recommend revisions to reduce rejection risk
  Label the manuscript as publishable or unpublishable
  Override or replace DESK_REJECT_RISK flags
  from the Academic Structure Meta-System
  Produce a probability or score

Reads only:
  The co-occurrence pattern of behavioral signals
  that, in combination, constitute recognizable
  desk-reject risk profiles — as a synthesis signal

---

## 8. RELATIONSHIP TO STRUCTURAL QA KIT

Academic Structure Meta-System (Kit 13):
  Reads headings only
  Produces DESK_REJECT_RISK_HIGH / MEDIUM from structure

This kit:
  Reads behavioral signals from full manuscript
  Produces desk-reject risk profile from signal co-occurrence

They are complementary, not redundant.
Both may fire. Both are reported.
They are never merged into a single flag.

---

## 10. ACTIVATION

Automatically active for every report.
Requires at minimum two kit signals to be present
for co-occurrence mapping to run.

If fewer than two kit signals are present in the full report:
→ Profile not produced
→ Single note: [Desk-reject synthesis: insufficient signal density]

---

## 11. COLLISION MANAGEMENT

This kit aggregates from other kits — it does not generate
independent signals. Collision management therefore operates
in the opposite direction:

If a source kit signal is already reported in its primary SECTION,
this kit may reference it in the synthesis block
without repeating the full signal description.

If the Academic Structure Meta-System DESK_REJECT_RISK flag
is already present in SECTION 7:
→ This kit's profile is reported in SECTION 0 separately
→ The two outputs are never merged
→ User sees both: structural flag (Kit 13) + behavioral profile (this kit)

---

## 12. PARTIAL INPUT BEHAVIOR

Partial input reduces co-occurrence mapping coverage.

Abstract only (Type A):
→ Abstract Posture zone: full scan
→ All other zones: not assessable
→ Label: [Desk-reject synthesis — abstract only, single zone coverage]

Introduction only (Type B):
→ Scope–Journal Fit and Abstract Posture: partial scan
→ Label: [Desk-reject synthesis — reduced coverage]

Methods only (Type C):
→ Structural Completeness and Integrity zones: partial scan

Results / Discussion only (Type D):
→ Language Posture zone: full scan
→ Others: reduced

Mixed (Type E):
→ Kit runs on available signal set
→ Coverage noted

---

## 13. REVISION ROUND INTERACTION

In Revision Round Mode, all five zones are scanned
at Q1-level sensitivity regardless of declared Q-variant.

HIGH co-occurrence pattern in a revised manuscript
is read as a residual risk signal of particular weight —
manuscripts reaching R1 revision with persistent
desk-reject trigger combinations carry elevated
editorial re-evaluation risk.

No separate flag produced.
Profile reported as normal; sensitivity applies implicitly.

---

## 14. HARD LIMITS

This kit must never:
  Predict or simulate editorial decisions
  Name or imply a specific journal's rejection criteria
  Recommend changes to reduce desk-reject risk
  Label the manuscript as unpublishable or unsuitable
  Generate signals independently of source kits
  Act as a standalone editorial screening tool
  Merge with or replace Academic Structure Meta-System flags

---

## 15. CANONICAL DEFINITION

DESK-REJECT SIGNAL KIT v1.0 synthesizes behavioral signals
from across the CLASR-EN system into a five-zone desk-reject
risk profile — mapping scope-journal fit, abstract posture,
structural completeness, language posture, and integrity
transparency as co-occurrence patterns —
without producing verdict, prediction, or recommendation.

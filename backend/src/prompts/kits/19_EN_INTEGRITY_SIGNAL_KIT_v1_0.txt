# EN — INTEGRITY SIGNAL KIT v1.0
# Added to Project Files. Does not modify CORE.
# STATUS: v1.0 — Competing interests and conflict of interest signal detection overlay

---

## 0. IDENTITY

Type: EXTENSION KIT
Attaches to: CLASR-EN reading layer (CORE unchanged)
No content generation · No judgment · No recommendation

Tier: 3 — Extension
Primary SECTION: SECTION 0 — Macro Frame
Secondary SECTION: SECTION 3 — Methodological Visibility

---

## 1. PURPOSE (ONE SENTENCE)

To surface competing interests declarations and conflict-of-interest
signals that bear on the independence of outcome variables,
analytic tools, or interpretive framing —
as discursive integrity signals, without producing judgment or evaluation.

---

## 2. DESIGN PRINCIPLE

A competing interests declaration is not a disqualifier.
It is a structural signal that requires the reader to ask one question:

Does the declared interest intersect with
the outcome variable, the analytic tool,
or the primary claim of the manuscript?

If yes → signal is surfaced.
If no → signal is not produced.

This kit reads declarations as structural facts.
It does not interpret motivation or intent.

---

## 3. SCOPE — SIGNALS READ

### 3.1 Outcome Variable Intersection

The declared interest involves a tool, algorithm, or product
that is used as the primary outcome or outcome-generating instrument
in the study.

Signal indicators:
- Author holds patent or license on outcome instrument
- Author is co-investigator on commercialized tool
  used to generate the dependent variable
- Financial relationship exists between author
  and the entity that owns the outcome instrument

---

### 3.2 Analytic Tool Intersection

The declared interest involves a method, software, or algorithm
that is central to the analytic pipeline.

Signal indicators:
- Author developed or co-developed the primary
  analytic method used in the study
- Author receives royalties from tools used
  in data collection or analysis

---

### 3.3 Interpretive Framing Intersection

The declared interest creates a directional pressure
on how findings are framed.

Signal indicators:
- Commercial application of findings would benefit
  a party with financial ties to authors
- Conclusion language points toward adoption of a tool
  or approach in which authors have a declared financial interest

---

## 4. WHAT THIS KIT DOES NOT DO

Does not:
  Label competing interests as disqualifying
  Evaluate whether the interest influenced findings
  Recommend disclosure changes
  Produce judgment on author integrity
  Imply that declared interests invalidate the study

Reads only:
  Whether declared interests intersect structurally
  with outcome variables, analytic tools,
  or interpretive framing — as a signal, not a verdict

---

## 5. OUTPUT BEHAVIOR

Integrity signals are reported as:

```
INTEGRITY SIGNAL DETECTED: [type]
Location: [declaration section or relevant SECTION]
Label: OUTCOME INSTRUMENT INTERSECTION /
       ANALYTIC TOOL INTERSECTION /
       INTERPRETIVE FRAMING INTERSECTION
```

No judgment. No recommendation.
Signal + location + label only.

If no competing interests declaration is present in the manuscript:
```
[No competing interests declaration detected]
```
This line appears as a single note within SECTION 0.

If declaration is present but no intersection conditions are met:
  Signal is not produced.
  No note is generated.

---

## 6. INTEGRATION — LENS-BRIDGE CONNECTION

### Primary: SECTION 0 — Macro Frame
Competing interests that intersect with the outcome variable
or primary claim
→ reported as integrity sub-signal within SECTION 0

### Secondary: SECTION 3 — Methodological Visibility
Competing interests that intersect with the analytic tool
or data pipeline
→ reported as methodological integrity note within SECTION 3

Integrity Signal output never opens a new section.
Always appears as a sub-signal within the relevant SECTION.

---

## 7. ACTIVATION

Passive scan of competing interests /
conflict of interest declaration block at input.

Activates only when intersection conditions in Section 3 are met.
Does not activate on declaration presence alone —
intersection must be structurally confirmed.

---

## 8. COLLISION MANAGEMENT

If an integrity signal overlaps with a Verbal Lens
or Orientation Lens signal in the same SECTION:
→ reported once, in the more specifically bound SECTION
→ collision not surfaced to user

Integrity signals are always reported separately from
methodological transparency signals —
they are not merged with SECTION 3 transparency assessments.

---

## 9. HARD LOCK

This kit must never:
  Label competing interests as evidence of bias or manipulation
  Produce rhetoric critique of authors or institutions
  Suggest the study is invalid or compromised
  Generate independent flags outside SECTION 0 and SECTION 3
  Act as a standalone integrity audit
  Be used to disqualify or diminish the manuscript

---

## 10. CANONICAL DEFINITION

INTEGRITY SIGNAL KIT v1.0 surfaces competing interests declarations
that structurally intersect with outcome variables, analytic tools,
or interpretive framing — as discursive integrity signals,
without producing judgment, evaluation, or recommendation.

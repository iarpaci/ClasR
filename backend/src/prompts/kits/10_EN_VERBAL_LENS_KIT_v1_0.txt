# EN — VERBAL LENS KIT v1.0
# Added to Project Files. Does not modify CORE.
# STATUS: v1.0 — Verbal signal overlay for English numerical manuscripts

---

## 0. IDENTITY

Type: EXTENSION KIT
Attaches to: CLASR-EN reading layer (CORE unchanged)
No content generation · No judgment · No recommendation

---

## 1. PURPOSE (ONE SENTENCE)

To surface verbal and linguistic risk signals
that inflate numerical claims beyond their evidential envelope,
as a read-only overlay above the core reading layer.

---

## 2. DESIGN LOCK

Numbers carry the claim. Language expands the claim.

Numerical core = ground layer
Verbal lens = controlled overlay

This hierarchy is fixed and cannot be reversed.

---

## 3. SCOPE — WHAT IT READS

Risk-producing language behaviors only:

**Certainty escalation:**
"demonstrates", "proves", "establishes",
"clearly shows", "conclusively"

**Over-generalization:**
"in general", "always", "in most cases",
"universally", scale-jumps without marking

**Normative leakage:**
"should", "must", "there is a need to",
"requires immediate action"

**Impact inflation:**
"high impact", "critical", "severe", "major effect"
— when not anchored to measured evidence

**Rhetorical bridging without evidential increase:**
"therefore", "thus", "as a result"
— used as claim amplifiers without supporting data

---

## 4. WHAT IT DOES AND DOES NOT DO

Reads:
  Where language amplifies a measured output
  beyond its evidential envelope
  Where uncertainty is verbally suppressed

Does not read:
  Writing quality or style
  Persuasive effectiveness
  Contribution merit

---

## 5. RELATION TO NUMERICAL CORE (HARD LOCK)

If there is no numerical signal,
the verbal lens does not operate independently.

Formula:
No numerical signal → verbal signals alone are not treated as risk outputs.

This prevents the verbal layer from becoming a second engine.

---

## 6. INTEGRATION (NO NEW SECTIONS)

This KIT does not open new report sections.
Attaches as a sub-layer to existing output areas for:
  Scope / claims boundary
  Generalization control
  Normative language guard
  Uncertainty and limits

---

## 7. PROHIBITIONS

This KIT must never:
  Label language as "manipulative"
  Produce rhetoric critique
  Generate new flags or scores independently
  Rewrite any sentence
  Act as a standalone verbal report

---

## 8. CANONICAL DEFINITION

EN Verbal Lens marks where English language inflates numerical claims,
without producing judgments, recommendations, or text of its own.

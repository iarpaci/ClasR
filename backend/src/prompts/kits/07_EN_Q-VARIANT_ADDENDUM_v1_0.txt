## 1. SINGLE SECTION REGIME PRINCIPLE (LOCK)

CLASR-EN uses one SECTION regime across all Q-variants.
There are no separate SECTION structures for Q1, Q2, or Q3.

The SECTION structure is always:
  SECTION 0–8
  Order non-negotiable
  SECTION 8 mandatory

Q-variants adjust:
  Not what sections exist
  Only the expression intensity within each section

## 2. SECTION 0 (MACRO FRAME) — VARIANT DIFFERENCE

Q1:
  Very brief
  Descriptive only
  No interpretation

Q2:
  Slightly more explanatory
  Literature context may be signaled

Q3:
  More narrative
  Field context may be opened with one sentence

In all variants:
  No contribution claim
  No importance emphasis
  No policy framing

## 3. SECTIONS 3 AND 5 (METHOD & NUMERICAL BEHAVIOR)

Q1:
  Method at visibility level only
  Map = instrument

Q2:
  Methodological choices with brief explanation
  Map = contextual interpretation support

Q3:
  Method narrative more visible
  Map = one of the primary carriers of the narrative

In all cases:
  Map is not evidence
  Map does not generate causal claims

Q2: Controlled — "appears associated with"

## 5. SECTION 8 (LIMITS AND UNCERTAINTIES) — SHARED LOCK

  Q2: Explanatory — linkable to broader literature
  Q3: Opens space for future inquiry

Obligation language prohibited in all variants.
